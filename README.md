Future data analyst
-------------------

Welcome to my GitHub! I am Asad Latif, a First-Class Honours graduate in Economics with Econometrics, with a strong focus on data analysis, econometric modeling, and data-driven decision-making. This repository is a collection of my projects demonstrating my proficiency in tools like Python, R, SQL, Power BI, and Excel. I have worked extensively on data manipulation, statistical analysis, automation, and creating interactive dashboards and visualizations. Through these projects, I aim to showcase my ability to turn raw data into actionable insights, supporting business strategy and decision-making. Feel free to explore my work and reach out for collaboration.
